How it works

Simple for everyone involved.

Whether you're requesting services or joining our team, we built this for clarity — not confusion.

01
Submit a request
Fill out our online form — date, time, location, setting type, and communication preferences (ASL, PSE, SEE, Tactile, and more).
02
We match & confirm
Our team matches your request with the most qualified available interpreter for your setting. You receive a confirmation with credentials.
03
Access & invoicing
Your interpreter arrives prepared and professional. Invoicing is transparent and itemized — you always know what you're paying for.
01
Apply & onboard
Submit your profile, credentials, and compliance documents. Our streamlined onboarding gets you on the roster quickly — no bureaucratic black holes.
02
Accept assignments
View available assignments that match your qualifications and availability. Accept, decline, or express interest — with full assignment details always visible.
03
Invoice & get paid
Submit invoices directly after each assignment. Fair, consistent pay with transparent rates — no surprises, no chasing down payments.
Why ASL Connection

Built from the inside out.

Most interpreter agencies are run by administrators. We're run by someone who grew up in Deaf culture, spent 20+ years as a working interpreter, and served our country in between.

✓
Interpreters are partners, not vendors. Respect, fair pay, and clear communication — always.
✓
Compliance built in. WA state requirements, HIPAA, educational settings — we know what's required and track it.
✓
Deaf-culture aware. We understand community dynamics, preferred communication modes, and why this work matters.
I founded ASL Connection because the Deaf community deserves more than a scheduling spreadsheet. They deserve an agency that understands their world — because it's my world too.
Simon Peters
Founder & Owner — CODA, Veteran, 20+ yr Interpreter
Services

Every setting, covered.

From elementary school IEPs to surgical suites, we place qualified interpreters across the full spectrum of assignments.

🎓
K–12 Educational
Classroom, IEP meetings, extracurriculars, and school events. Compliant with OSPI and district fingerprinting requirements.
🏥
Medical & Healthcare
Appointments, procedures, mental health, and hospital stays. HIPAA-compliant interpreters with appropriate immunization documentation.
⚖️
Legal Settings
Depositions, hearings, consultations, and courtroom proceedings. Certified interpreters experienced in legal terminology.
🏛️
Postsecondary
University lectures, advising, campus events, and disability services programs at colleges and universities.
🤝
Business & Corporate
Meetings, trainings, conferences, and HR processes. Professional interpreters who understand business environments.
🌍
Community & Events
Religious services, community events, theatrical performances, and platform interpreting. All communication modes available.

Available modes: ASL · PSE · SEE · Tactile · Pro-Tactile · Sign-to-Voice · CDI · Trilingual
